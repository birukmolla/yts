Our Services

At Yetwlid Tesfa School (YTS), we offer a wide range of services designed to support the academic and personal growth of our students from nursery through grade 12, including kindergarten. Our comprehensive educational programs are tailored to meet the diverse needs of our learners, ensuring that each child receives a well-rounded education.

Our services include a robust curriculum that emphasizes core subjects, extracurricular activities, and technology integration. We also provide specialized support such as tutoring, counseling, and enrichment programs to help students excel academically and socially. Our dedicated staff collaborates closely with families, fostering a supportive community that enhances the learning experience.

In addition to academic services, we offer various resources and facilities, including a modern library, science labs, and sports programs, all aimed at promoting holistic development. At YTS, we are committed to preparing our students for future success by providing them with the tools, opportunities, and support they need to thrive. Discover the many ways we can serve your family and help your child reach their full potential!
