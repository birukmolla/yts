About Us

At Yetwlid Tesfa School, we are dedicated to providing a comprehensive and nurturing educational experience for children from nursery through grade 12, including kindergarten. Founded on the principles of excellence, respect, and inclusivity, our school is committed to fostering a safe and engaging learning environment.

Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to become responsible and successful individuals. We believe in a balanced approach to education, integrating academic rigor with creative expression and emotional development. Our experienced and passionate educators work closely with each student, offering personalized guidance and support to help them thrive.

At YTS, we embrace innovation and technology, ensuring our students are equipped with the tools they need for a rapidly changing world. We encourage collaboration, critical thinking, and problem-solving, preparing our students to meet the challenges of the future.

Join us at Yetwlid Tesfa School, where we are not just educating students, but inspiring future leaders and lifelong learners. Together, we can build a brighter tomorrow!
